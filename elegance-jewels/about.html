Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Elegance Jewelry

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995 by master jeweler Elizabeth Morgan, Elegance Jewelry began as a small boutique in the heart of New York City. With a passion for creating beautiful, timeless pieces and an unwavering commitment to quality, Elizabeth quickly established a reputation for excellence in the jewelry industry.

What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a renowned jewelry brand, but our core values remain the same. Every piece in our collection is still crafted with the same attention to detail and dedication to quality that Elizabeth instilled from day one.

Today, Elegance Jewelry is led by Elizabeth's daughter, Sophia, who continues her mother's legacy while bringing her own modern vision to the brand. Under Sophia's guidance, we've expanded our collections while staying true to our heritage of exceptional craftsmanship.
