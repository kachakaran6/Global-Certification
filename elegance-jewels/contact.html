Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom jewelry design services?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom pieces tailored to your preferences. Please contact us directly to discuss your ideas and schedule a consultation with our design team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original packaging. Custom pieces are non-returnable. Please see our full return policy for more details.

Do you provide jewelry appraisals?

Yes, we offer professional appraisal services for insurance purposes. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

How do I care for my jewelry?

We recommend storing your jewelry in a cool, dry place and cleaning it regularly with a soft cloth. For specific care instructions based on the type of jewelry, please refer to our care guide.
