How It All Began

Cafe Chronicles was founded in 2010 by James and Emma Wilson, two coffee enthusiasts with a dream of creating a space where quality coffee could be celebrated.

What started as a small cart in the local farmers' market has grown into a beloved coffee brand with a commitment to quality, sustainability, and community.

Our journey has been fueled by a passion for exceptional coffee and the connections it creates between people.
